About SetListify

How we're making setlist management easier for musicians everywhere

Uhh, this is SetListify

How we saved time and hope to save yours

I am Doriello from The Creaks. We always use set lists for live performances, as do you, likely. We happened to stumble over a certain issue though - as we were playing more and more often, we wanted to give our fans a different show every time, sometimes the venue wanted a shorter or longer show, sometimes we wanted to add some mid-show commentary and it soon became a hassle to make a new set list every time with different order and new ideas.

The Solution

From frustration to innovation

That's when SetListify came to mind. I quickly developed a prototype and was amazed with how modular our set lists became. We can now plan our shows remotely or together, we can reorder tracks on the fly, add mid-show notes, we can print our set-lists out, share them, analyze them and just overall enjoy the process much more.

For Musicians, By Musicians

Sharing with the community

I decided to share it with the world, because I believe musicians must help each other, playing shows is difficult enough - let's make it easier. SetListify is built with the real needs of performing artists in mind, because we've experienced those needs firsthand.
